About Me

I was born at Baptist-Desoto in Southaven,MS, weighing 7lbs and 14oz. I wasn't a noisy infant nor a quiet infant, I just didn't bother anybody. When I was 5 years old, my great grandmother actually held me back from fun activites with others because she was afraid that mosquitos would eat me alive.

Even today, I never consider being in a group or group session with others because it's just how I was raised. I always worked alone and loved being independent and figuring out the problem on my own. I was always disciplined for laughing at something that my cousins and brother did and sometimes I just got little taps on the hand for something they did!

I'm really a quiet and silent person until I get around people that I know well. I get tangled when it comes to explaining something and how it works. I struggle with processing information when someone asks me to do something because of a seizure disorder called Epilepsy. My brain actually slows down often, so I have to ask that person or people what did they said because I may not understand it the first time they say it or I might not prepare it correctly. I take medication for this, so thanks be to God, my seizures are under control. Without that medication, I wouldn't be sitting here today. I'm only following the doctor's and God's orders as instructed.

I'm VEERRRY nervous when standing in front of enormous crowds because I just feel claustrophobic. It's a possibility I might get over it, but I won't guarantee it! The worst thing for me is presenting and explaining presentations. It's a BIG communication problem.

I also enjoy playing piano when I have free time available. I love playing keyboard because its just enjoyable to listen, record, and try to play a song you like very much, and if you know the songs and the notes, it's even better. I don't know the notes. There's another way of playing and thats just playing by what you hear.
